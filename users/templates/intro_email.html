Your Guide to Acuity

We use Acuity Scheduling to keep your training sessions organized and efficient. This tool helps us manage attendance, preventing overcrowding and ensuring each athlete gets the attention they deserve. Go to this website, OptimumAthletesBooking.as.me to schedule your throwing, hitting and lifting. This link is also available in our Instagram Bio.

Your customized training program is crafted on TRAQ (traq.drivelinebaseball.com). Log in daily to stay informed about your scheduled activities, ensuring you're ready for each session. YOUR TRAQ ACCOUNT WILL BE CREATED ON YOUR FIRST DAY AT THE FACILITY WITH A TRAINER. PLEASE DO NOT CREATE AN ACCOUNT ON YOUR OWN.

We are combining the power of Acuity and TRAQ to enhance your training experience. Acuity makes scheduling seamless, ensuring organized and focused sessions. Meanwhile, TRAQ provides your personalized training plan, accessible daily. Together, they streamline your fitness journey, making it efficient and tailored just for you.

This is an example of what TRAQ will look like when your account is created. Green is throwing, blue is hitting, and orange is lifting.

When scheduling for throwing, go into Acuity and find the type of day that you need to sign up for. In this example, “Standard” throwing falls under “Non Velocity Throwing” in Acuity. If your TRAQ ever says something like “ Mound Blend B” then you will sign up for “Bullpen” in Acuity. You can see all the different types of throwing days underneath the “Throwing” category.

For Hitting and Lifting, you will always just sign up for “Hitting” or “Lifting” in Acuity. There is no specific name you need to sign up for no matter what your TRAQ says.

Here is an example of what a day would look like based off of the screenshot above:

  1. TRAQ says you have “Standard” throwing so you book “Non Velocity Throwing” for 4:30 pm
  2. There is an open hitting slot at 5:00 pm so you book “Hitting”
  3. You book “Lifting” at 6:00 pm following your hitting

When you have a day where you are throwing, hitting, and lifting we ask that you guys schedule the lifting last so when you are at your physical peak in the day throwing and hitting. Hitting slots fill up quickly, so we suggest that you schedule your days based off of the availability for hitting.
